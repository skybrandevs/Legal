About us.

Conscious of our reputation, which has been built over a period of over 59 years we strive to achieve the highest level of professionalism.

OLAOTAN AJOSE-ADEOGUN & CO (The Rose Chambers) was founded by the present principal in chambers Mr. Olaotan Ajose-Adeogun as a re-establishment of O.Ajose-Adeogun & Co which dates back to 1961 as a full service Nigerian law firm distinguished by its premium service. We moved to the present Lagos location in January 1991 and went on to open a branch in Port Harcourt. There are plans to also open an office in Abuja because we understand that for us to provide better professional service to our clients at a competitive cost and connect with clients on every level we must get as close to them as we can. Our aim is to provide the services of seasoned attorneys who combine their diverse experience and professionalism to pursue optimal results in every case.with the dynamism required to satisfy the constantly evolving dynamism of law. We are dedicated to securing the rights of individuals, families and businesses in critical legal matters.

With vast experience defending in Corporate Law, Commercial Practice including Law of Contract, Law of Real Property including Law of LandLord and Tenant and the acquisition of property, Banking and Insurance, Debt Recovery, Immigration Law, Labour Law including industrial disputes, Energy & Petroleum Law, Arbitration Law and Proceedings, and General Litigation Practice we can help you understand your rights, ensure that you meet your legal requirements, and advocate for your interests.

We care deeply about successful outcomes for our clients; we are dedicated to working hard for you in every way, and we take great pride in delivering thorough, personal service. Experienced. Confident. Dedicated. Reliable. Results driven. These are just a few of the words clients use to describe OLAOTAN AJOSE-ADEOGUN & CO (The Rose Chambers). With more than 59 years of combined experience, we have provided representation to people across Nigeria, managing cases that range from typical to highly complex and securing litigation judgements in all the higher courts of Nigeria.

Our clientele spans across the petroleum Industry, Banking and Insurance, shipping Industry and several individual and corporate clients in Nigeria and abroad. Our library ensures that we keep in touch with materials, law reports and precedent books that guide relevant practice. It’s in our policy to ensure that the library is continuously updated with relevant materials that are acquired locally and abroad.

Our Principal & his team

The present principal in chambers, Mr. Olaotan Ajose-Adeogun was called to the Nigerian bar in 1984. Following his call to the bar, he served as junior counsel in the chambers of Professor A. B. Kasunmu, a Senior Advocate of Nigeria (SAN). It was here that he was exposed to litigation practice and corporate law at the highest levels.

Mr. Olaotan Ajose-Adeogun worked for Professor Kasunmu (SAN) for a period of five and a half years. In his time at Professor Kasunmu’s chambers, a new foundation was laid for the re-engineering of the old firm O.Ajose Adeogun & Co. This exercise commenced in 1988 with the formal registration and change of the firm name to OLAOTAN AJOSE - ADEOGUN & CO (The Rose Chambers).

The original firm O.Ajose Adeogun & Co was established in 1961 by Mr. Olatunji AjoseAdeogun who was called to the English bar (middle temple) in 1952. On his arrival from England he practiced with the law firm Thomas, Williams and Kayode, after which he took up employment with the L.E.D.B, which is now the present-day Lagos State Development and Property Corporation. He remained in private legal practice until his elevation as a Judge of the High Court of Lagos State in 1975. In 1985 he was further elevated to the Court of Appeal from where he voluntarily retired in 1987.

Mr Olaotan Ajose-Adeogun is a member of The Chartered Institute of Arbitrators England, a fellow of the Nigerian Institute of Chartered Arbitrators, a member of the Lagos Court of Arbitration and a Notary Public for Nigeria having been so appointed by the Chief Justice of Nigeria in 1993. We also have present in chambers Mrs. Duro-Emmanuel (formally Miss Ayo Ajose-Adeogun) called in 1978 and 5 junior counsel / solicitors working for the firm. The firm has support staff such as a chambers receptionist, chambers accountant, litigation clerk, cleaners & security. Mr Olaotan AjoseAdeogun has over the past 36 years acquired vast experience in corporate law, commercial practice including law of contract, law of real property including law of landlord and tenant and the acquisition of property, banking and Insurance, debt recovery, Immigration law, labour law including industrial disputes, energy & petroleum law, arbitration law and proceedings, and general litigation practice.
