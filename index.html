About Us

OLAOTAN AJOSE-ADEOGUN & CO.

This law firm was originally established in 1961 by Mr. Olatunji Ajose-Adeogun who was called to the English Bar (Middle Temple) in 1952. On his arrival from England he practiced with the law firm Thomas, Williams and Kayode, after which he took up employment with the L.E.D.B, which is now the present-day Lagos State Development and Property Corporation. Mr. Olatunji Ajose-Adeogun established the law firm O. AJOSE –ADEOGUN & CO. in 1961 and remained in private legal practice until his elevation as a Judge of the High Court of Lagos State in 1975. In 1985 he was further elevated to the Court of Appeal from where he voluntarily retired in 1987.
